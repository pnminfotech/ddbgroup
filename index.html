Who we are?

DDB Group builds new homes with quality craftsmanship, exceptional design, and Perfect service in Chakan, Pune and the surrounding area. With DDB Builders, you’ve got a team of professionals handling the work behind you. We’ll guide you through the design, permits, construction, and financing of your dream home.
