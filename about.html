Welcome to DDB Group

DDB Group builds new homes with quality craftsmanship, exceptional design, and Perfect service in Chakan, Pune and the surrounding area. With DDB Builders, you’ve got a team of professionals handling the work behind you. We’ll guide you through the design, permits, construction, and financing of your dream home.

With more than five years of experience, you can enjoy the process of creating a place to match your lifestyle. we stand behind every home we build. Like the many customers before you, we are ready to help you fall in love with your new home.

Established in the year 2017 as a Construction Developer at Chakan, Pune. We work with both residential and commercial clients, and customize every job for our client’s unique need and preferences. We Focuses on attaining the best possible results by following the required set processes and constantly moulding them. The ultimate long term goal of the DDB Group is not only to grow by scale but also to deliver superior quality for better living which adds value to customer.
